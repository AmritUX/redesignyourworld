Rebranding and redesigning Lotus Dancers' website


One of my most favourite project, because I was given the opportunity to leave an impact on women’s life in the world of dance. Gauri is a dance-teacher who owns a dance school in Basel, Switzerland. I redesigned the client’s website, digital presence and branding guidelines.

The Problem


  • The previous website didn’t reflect a dance school but merely a personal website of a dance instructor.
  • Gauri's dance school was not established as a recognizable organization.
  • Gauri's brand did not have a solid digital presence.




User Research


  • Students and non-students missed a connection with Gauri’s brand.
  • Students felt that there was no pride associated with Gauri's brand
  • Potential students found the older website unclear and difficult to understand.
  • Many dance schools’ websites and online presence were too much focused on the teacher and not the students.







The Solutions


  • Gauri's brand transitioned from a personal brand to a business brand named the Lotus Dancers.
  • User-centred guidelines was established across instagram, facebook and the newly created Lotus Dancers website.
  • The lotus dancers website was created with the students in mind. As the website has now clear-to-understand tabs, information and a call for action for the recruitment of potential students.
