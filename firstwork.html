Designing a member portal for First Work


To design a member portal for First Work. The main purpose of the portal is to create and maintain communication between First Work's members.

The portal serves to inform and update members of First Work's events, activities, research reports and webinars.

The member portal was designed in collaboration with Greyscale Consulting.

Hosting Platform:
 Wild Apricot

Website:
 firstwork.org




The Problem


  • Overflow and scattered files from First Work.
  • Events, webinars, bulletins and other First Work’s activities were hosted on separate online platforms which caused issues to retrieve, read and download information by the user.
  • Evaluating and setting up the flow of the member portal.
  • Selecting the right online platform that could host the member portal.



User Research


  • The members mainly consist of youth employment service provider and would need a portal where info is relevant to their niche.
  • The members would like to have all their regional agenda and minutes easily accessable in the portal.
  • The member desires to download presentations of conferences, webinars and events.
  • Wild Apricot member management software is the best suitable software for the portal because of user-centred features, reasonable price and ease of use for both First Work’s staff and members.



The Solutions


  • Wild Apricot was decided for the member portal because the software is the one-solution platform where all of First Work’s activities can be hosted including online payment and registration.
  • Designed a communication model where members can stay up-to-date on First Work’s involvements, activities and news.
  • Members can retrieve and read all information regarding youth employment in the member portal oppose to looking for information on the internet.
